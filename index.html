About TapTapTap

TapTapTap is a texting game aimed toward improving the texting proficiency of Android users in a fun and exciting way. Using Correct Gross Words A Minute (CGWAM), users can analyze their texting ability and improve their skills.



Choose from three
different game types

Can either be Single Word (a game with a single word), Multiple Words (a game with a few words), or Paragraph (a game with a LOT of words.

Add your own words or phrases

Add your own words or phrases to the game types of your choice! They can be words that you typically have difficulty writing, or phrases that are fun to type!

Check out your high scores!

Routinely check out your previous high scores and montior your progress in each game type. The high the score, the better you're getting!

Found a bug?

We love bugs! If you find any bugs with our game we definately want to know so please email us at taptaptapdev@gamil.com and we will get right on it!
